Delete Your Account

Last updated: July 2, 2026

ELEMENT | 08 works fully offline and needs no account. If you created an optional free account (for cloud backup and coach↔athlete features), this page explains how to permanently delete that account and all of its associated data.

// IN THE APP (RECOMMENDED)

The fastest way is directly in the app:

  • Open ELEMENT | 08 and sign in.
  • Go to Settings › Account › Delete account & data.
  • Confirm. Your account and all of its cloud data are removed immediately. This cannot be undone.

// BY EMAIL (IF YOU NO LONGER HAVE THE APP)

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot sign in, email apnea.advantage@gmail.com from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We verify the request against that address and complete the deletion within 30 days.

// WHAT GETS DELETED

Deleting your account permanently removes everything we store for it on our servers, including:

  • Your email address, chosen display name, and coach/athlete role.
  • Your cloud logbook backup.
  • Coaching connections and anything shared through them (assigned plans you received, plan completions, notes, any logbook sessions you attached, and the PBs/goals in your athlete profile).

// WHAT IS NOT AFFECTED

  • Data on your device: your local training logbook stays on your device. To remove it, delete individual sessions in the app, or uninstall the app (which removes all local data).
  • Purchases & subscriptions: records of App Store / Google Play purchases are held by Apple and Google, not by us. Manage or cancel subscriptions through your App Store or Google Play account.

// CONTACT

Questions about account or data deletion? Contact us at apnea.advantage@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
