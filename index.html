// FEATURES
// 01
Free Session Builder
CO2 and O2 tables, breathwork mode, custom rest/hold/recover blocks with full audio announcements.
// 02
Logbook
Every session saved with SpO2/HR charts, block timeline, contractions, and personal notes. Import dive logs from several dive computer brands.
// 03
Training Plans & Season Planning
Follow structured programs from beginner to advanced, or build your own season plan with periodized phases for competition prep.
// 04
Insights & Analytics
Hold time trends, depth bands, SpO2 exposure zones, and live SpO2, HR, and PI readings via Bluetooth oximeter. Detailed analysis after every session.
// FOR COACHES

Build training plans in your spreadsheet, share them with students as a single file. Students import in one tap. No accounts. No subscriptions. Imported plans show "Imported from {your name}" and the author field is locked once you export.
