// Why metered
Real compute, real cost

Every question runs a live AI model that we pay for each time it is used, unlike the rest of the app. A monthly pool keeps it fair: heavy users cover their own cost, and everyone else is not subsidising them through a higher flat price.

Your data stays yours

Only your question and summarised figures, such as averages, counts, and per-hold heart-rate and oxygen stats, are sent to answer it. Your raw dives, full depth profiles, location and notes never leave your device. You can limit what the assistant sees, or turn it off entirely.

// Questions
What counts as one “assistant question”?

One question you ask and the answer it gives back. Follow-ups in the same conversation each count as a question. The charts the app draws for you are free.

Do unused questions roll over?

No. Your allowance resets at the start of each calendar month. If you need more before then, add a one-time top-up.

I have Lifetime Pro — do I get the assistant?

Lifetime Pro includes every app feature forever, plus the same 10 assistant questions a month that everyone gets. For heavier use, add the Assistant add-on or a one-time top-up. The assistant runs on a live, paid AI service, so it stays a separate subscription rather than part of the one-time price.

Is my dive data private?

Yes. Your raw logs stay on your device; only aggregated numbers are sent to answer a question, to a processor under EU-standard data protection. Full detail is in our Privacy Policy.

Is the assistant medical or safety advice?

No. It is an informational tool for exploring your own logged data. It is not medical, safety, or dive-planning advice, and you should never let it influence how you dive. See our Terms.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Manage or cancel in your App Store or Google Play account. You keep access until the end of the period you have paid for.
